ABOUT

The person behind the product

I like problems with too many moving parts.

I’m Vidhi, a Product Designer with a front-end foundation. My best work lives inside complex products—multiple roles, permissions, forms, statuses and dependencies—where clarity has to be designed, not decorated.

I ask what changes for the user, what the business needs to protect, and what engineering can realistically build. Then I turn the answers into journeys, interface patterns and specifications people can act on.

Outside screens, I paint with acrylics and build things by hand. It keeps my visual eye sharp—and reminds me that the strongest ideas often begin messy.
